# MeetScribe — Sales and Outreach Guide

## Product Pitch (One Liner)

MeetScribe turns meeting recordings into structured meeting minutes and ready-to-export tickets — in 5 minutes, not 2 hours.

---

## The Core Message

Every meeting produces decisions, action items, and requirements. After the meeting, someone has to write it all up and create tickets manually. MeetScribe eliminates that work — upload the recording, AI extracts everything, review in minutes, export to your board.

**Two outputs from one recording:**
1. Meeting minutes to share with the team
2. Tickets created directly in Azure DevOps

## Demo Script (3 Minutes)

Use this when someone agrees to see a demo:

**Setup:** Have a pre-recorded meeting result ready in MeetScribe (something with user stories, action items, and decisions extracted).

```
[0:00 - 0:30] The Problem
"Let me show you what happens after a typical 1-hour sprint planning meeting.
Normally, you'd spend 45-60 minutes writing up the minutes and creating tickets.
With MeetScribe, watch what happens."

[0:30 - 1:00] Upload and Extract
"I uploaded this recording. AI processed it in about 2 minutes.
Here's what it extracted — user stories, action items, decisions, risks.
All structured. All from one recording."

[1:00 - 1:30] Document View
"Look at this — confidence scores tell me what to trust and what to check.
Green means high confidence. Yellow means review it.
And this — click the source — that's the exact words from the meeting.
If someone says 'I never said that' — here's the proof."

[1:30 - 2:15] Export
"Now I want these as tickets. Click Export to Azure DevOps.
I pick my project, pick the sprint.
Each card is editable — I can change the title, description, priority, acceptance criteria.
This checkbox lets me include or exclude specific items.
Click Create."

[2:15 - 2:45] Results
"Done. 8 tickets created. Each one shows the ticket ID and a link.
I can click 'Open in DevOps' and see the actual work item.
It has the description, acceptance criteria, and source quote included."

[2:45 - 3:00] Summary
"That took 3 minutes total. Normally, this is an hour of manual work.
Meeting minutes are ready to share. Tickets are on the board.
Nothing lost. Everything traceable."
```
